Welcome to this week's Top 7, where the DEV editorial team handpicks their favorite posts from the previous week.

@katcosgrove examines the case of Ingress NGINX, a utility that roughly 50% of cloud-native environments depend on, and what its trajectory reveals about how businesses think about the open source projects underneath their products. The stakes may turn out to be higher than most companies realize.
