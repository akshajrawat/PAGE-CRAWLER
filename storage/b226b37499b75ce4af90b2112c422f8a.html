We empower hackers.

Major League Hacking (MLH) helps developers learn by doing.

mlh.com Global Joined on May 4, 2020 hi@mlh.io

MLH is the largest developer community in the world. Since 2013, we've empowered more than 5 million developers across nearly 100 countries to learn new technologies, build creative projects, and launch their careers — all through hands-on programs like hackathons, fellowships, workshops, and online challenges and content.

In February 2026, MLH acquired DEV — bringing together the place where developers build with the place where developers share. If DEV is the library, MLH is the lab.
