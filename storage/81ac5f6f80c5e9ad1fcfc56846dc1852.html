Share₂Fedi is an instance-agnostic share page for the Fediverse. With it, you can post to various federated platforms from a single page.

Supported projects:

  • Mastodon (incl. Hometown, Fedibird, GlitchCafé)
  • Misskey (incl. Firefish, Calckey, FoundKey, Meisskey)
  • Friendica
  • Hubzilla
  • GNU Social

Share₂Fedi is developed and maintained by Nikita Karamov. Source code is on GitHub. Status page.

Privacy Notice

When you click the ‘Publish’ button, you’ll get redirected to a Fediverse instance you’ve specified. It may process and/or store your data. Please refer to the privacy policy of the respective instance.
