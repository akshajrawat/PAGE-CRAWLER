Congrats to the Algolia Agent Studio Challenge Winners!

#algoliachallenge #devchallenge #agents

The results are in! Thank you all for your patience.

We saw a wide range of submissions and are thrilled to announce our winners for the Algolia Agent Studio Challenge. While we wish we could recognize every submission, today we're highlighting the winners that stood out the most.

See if you made the list!

Congratulations To…

Consumer-Facing Non-Conversational Experiences

@_boweii introduced The Refusal Engine, a proactive security tool built to prevent cloud misconfigurations before they ever reach deployment. It’s a highly practical agent that works in the background.

Prizes

Our four winners will each receive the following:

  • $750 USD
  • DEV++ Membership
  • Exclusive DEV Badge

All Participants with a valid submission will receive a completion badge on their DEV profile to recognize their hard work!

Our Sponsor

We want to give a big shout out to Algolia for partnering with us on this challenge. Their Agent Studio platform makes it incredibly intuitive to build, test, and deploy sophisticated AI agents.

Join their Spring Edition of Algolia DevBit on April 1, featuring OpenAI: 90 minutes of short talks, live demos, and practical insights on building agentic workflows.

Register for Algolia DevBit

This edition will be online, but also live from their Paris office. If you’re nearby, select the in-person ticket when you register and join their team IRL for food and drinks!

What’s next?

Check out our challenge page to see what’s launching next, and follow the challenge tag so you don’t miss any announcements:

#devchallenge

This is the official tag for submissions and announcements related to DEV Challenges.

Thank you to everyone who participated! We hope you had fun, felt challenged, and added something impressive to your professional portfolio.

See you next time!
