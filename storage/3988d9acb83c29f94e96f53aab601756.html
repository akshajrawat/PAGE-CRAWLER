Do you have any New Year resolutions or goals?

#discuss #career #accountability

I don't typically set resolutions but I do have a work-in-progress "2026 Goals" bingo card that I've been mulling over these last few days.

I'm not sharing the full details because it contains lots of personal stuff but I will share that one of spaces is occupied with "prepare a talk and present at a local toastmasters club". I am hoping that 2026 will be a year where I can start doing some public speaking at dev events, but I'd really like to build up some more confidence before I approach it all.

Anyway, I first learned about and felt inspired by a bingo format from following @eevajonnapanula. You can learn more about it from their 2025 wishes that they shared with the community:

2025, Please, Be Gentle

I really love not having to whittle down my hopes and dreams into a small subset of goals which means approaching my year with more flexibility. I have several hunches on how things may shift and this allows me to follow my interests and evolving priorities as the year unfolds.

I think I'll also just be happy if I can cross out five of my goals without actually getting a bingo, but a bingo would be pretty cool!

Care to share any of your goals?!
