Congrats to the Winners of the "Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge" Presented by Mux!

#muxchallenge #devchallenge #showdev

The results are in.

From solar system navigators to CLIs that manage software EOLs to cancer screening tools, there was no shortage of incredible projects shared in our Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge Presented by Mux.

We absolutely loved seeing the community showcase their projects and share the stories behind what they built. This is a challenge we hope to bring back in the future so that folks have a regular opportunity to highlight their work and practice their pitching skills!

As always, we wish we could recognize every submission. Regardless of whether or not you win, we hope you are proud of what you accomplished. Thank you for participating and sharing you projects with us. ❤️

Read on for the winners!

Congratulations To…

Overall Winner

@marcelfahle created BOLD, a video search platform that solves a problem anyone with a large video library knows too well: you can't find anything.
