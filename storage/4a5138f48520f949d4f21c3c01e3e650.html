7 Free AI Website Tools For Everyone and Anyone

#webdev #javascript #beginners #productivity

AI tools are on the rise and they are becoming increasingly popular for everyone, especially web content creators. With AI writing tools, it's easier than ever to create content that is both accurate and engaging.There are several free AI tools available online to help you create content quickly and with minimal effort.

The internet has become an essential part of our lives, and websites have become the primary means of online communication for businesses, organizations, and individuals. Building a website is only the first step in creating an online presence, and there are many aspects to consider to ensure it meets its objectives. Luckily, there are numerous free website tools available, powered by artificial intelligence, that can help improve website performance, design, and user experience. In this context, we will explore seven AI free website tools that everyone can use to take their website to the next level.

ScribeHow


ScribeHow is a recently launched application that lets you record a process and instantly generate a text and picture-based step-by-step guide.
The app works by recording your screen and noting any inputs you make during your recording. For example, the app will record where you click and what keys you press and generate a screenshot and text-based documentation. While the auto-generated text doesn’t fill in all the details, it comes pretty close, and you can always edit the text to be more specific.

Overall, ease of use is not an issue. You don’t need to know a lick of programming. In fact, you barely need any technical chops at all. The app is entirely “point and click.”

Grammarly


Grammarly is a free online grammar checker that helps you write better English. It is a free tool that can be used to check your grammar, spelling, and punctuation. It can also be used to check your writing style and tone. It is a great tool for writers, students, and anyone who wants to improve their writing skills.
It has various features that make it easy to use. It has a built-in dictionary that allows you to look up words and phrases. It also has a spell checker that can help you correct spelling mistakes. It also has a grammar checker that can help you correct grammar mistakes. It also has a thesaurus that can help you find synonyms for words.

Poised

It is an AI-powered video analysis tool designed to provide feedback on your public speaking skills. The website allows you to upload a video of yourself giving a presentation or speech, and then analyzes various aspects of your delivery, such as pace, energy, and body language, and provides feedback on areas for improvement. The platform is designed to help individuals improve their communication skills and become more confident public speakers.is an AI-powered video analysis tool designed to provide feedback on your public speaking skills. The website allows you to upload a video of yourself giving a presentation or speech, and then analyzes various aspects of your delivery, such as pace, energy, and body language, and provides feedback on areas for improvement. The platform is designed to help individuals improve their communication skills and become more confident public speakers.

Lumen5

Lumen5 is a free online video creation tool that allows you to create videos from text, images, and audio. It is a web-based platform that enables users to create video content for marketing and other purposes. It provides a simple drag-and-drop interface, a library of stock media, and AI-powered tools to help create videos quickly and easily, without the need for prior video production experience.

DALL·E


DALL·E is an AI model that can generate images from text description using a technique called "image generation." It is a free tool that can be used to create images from text. It is a web-based platform that enables users to create images from text. It provides a simple drag-and-drop interface, a library of stock media, and AI-powered tools to help create images quickly and easily, without the need for prior video production experience. It also provides various functionalities like variation of same image, image search, and image editing.

Lalal.ai

It is one of interesting tools . Sometimes you may have listened to a song and you want to know the lyrics of that song or want o listen to ay particular vocals only then this tool is for you .

Lalal.ai is a website that offers an AI-powered music separation service. It allows users to upload an audio file (such as a song), and then uses machine learning algorithms to separate the different audio tracks within the song, such as the vocals, drums, and guitar. This can be useful for a variety of purposes, such as creating remixes, removing vocals for karaoke tracks, or isolating certain elements for analysis or creative experimentation. Lalal.ai uses advanced audio processing technology to achieve high-quality results and has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to use even for those without extensive audio engineering experience.

Sembly

Sembly.ai is a website that provides AI-powered meeting transcription and analysis tools for businesses and organizations. It uses advanced speech recognition algorithms to transcribe audio and video recordings of meetings in real-time, and then provides a range of features to help users analyze and extract insights from those transcriptions. Some of the key features of Sembly.ai include the ability to search and filter transcripts, add notes and annotations, and generate action items and summaries. The platform also provides analytics and metrics to help users track key performance indicators related to their meetings, such as meeting duration, participant engagement, and more. Sembly.ai is designed to help businesses and organizations save time and improve productivity by making it easier to manage and analyze meetings, and to help ensure that all participants are on the same page.
